## What Is This Course?

A **6-hour, 15-module** self-paced course that teaches Product Managers how to use Microsoft 365 Copilot to save 14+ hours per week on everyday PM work — meeting notes, user research synthesis, PRD writing, data analysis, communications, and more.

## Who Is This For?

- **Product Managers** and **Product Owners** at any experience level
- No coding or AI expertise required
- You should be comfortable using Microsoft 365 apps (Word, Excel, Teams, Outlook)

## How to Complete Exercises

Each module follows the same pattern:

1. **Read the module** — understand the concept and see the example prompt
2. **Open Copilot** — go to [copilot.microsoft.com](https://copilot.microsoft.com) (or use `Alt + Spacebar`)
3. **Copy the prompt** from the module
4. **Paste into Copilot** — if the exercise requires files, upload them first
5. **Compare your output** to the example — Copilot outputs vary, so focus on structure and quality rather than exact wording

> **Tip:** Don't just copy-paste. Read the prompt to understand *why* it's structured that way — this builds your prompting skills.

## FAQ / Troubleshooting

**I don't have a Copilot license. Can I still take the course?**
You can read all modules and understand the concepts, but you won't be able to complete the hands-on exercises. Ask your IT team about getting a Microsoft 365 Copilot Enterprise license.

**Copilot gave me different output than the example. Is that wrong?**
No — Copilot outputs vary every time. Focus on whether your output has the same *structure* and *quality* as the example, not the exact same words.

**My progress reset. What happened?**
Progress is stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing browser data or switching browsers will reset it. There's no account-based sync.

**Can I skip modules?**
Level 1 modules build on each other, so we recommend going in order. Levels 2 and 3 are standalone — skip to whatever interests you.

**Where are the CSV files? I can't find them on the website.**
CSV files aren't viewable on the website. Download them from the [sample-files/data folder on GitHub](https://github.com/sanjaygupta-professional/copilot-pm-course/tree/main/sample-files/data), then upload to Copilot or open in Excel.

**How long does the full course take?**
About 6 hours if you complete all exercises. You can spread it over multiple days.
