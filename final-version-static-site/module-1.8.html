Module 1.8: Copilot in Microsoft Teams

Duration: 25 minutes Prerequisites: Modules 1.1-1.7 completed, Teams with Copilot enabled

What You'll Learn

By the end of this module, you will:

  1. Use Copilot to get meeting summaries and action items
  2. Catch up on meetings you missed
  3. Use Copilot in Teams chats for quick assistance
  4. Leverage Copilot in channels for collaboration
  5. Save hours on meeting follow-ups and team communication

Why Teams + Copilot Matters for PMs

PMs spend a significant portion of their time in meetings and team communication:

Activity Typical Time/Week With Copilot
Meeting notes & follow-ups 3-4 hours 30 min
Catching up on missed meetings 1-2 hours 15 min
Reading long chat threads 1 hour 10 min
Total Savings ~5 hours/week

Section 1: Meeting Intelligence

Copilot in Teams can summarize meetings, extract action items, and help you catch up on discussions.

Accessing Meeting Summaries

After a Teams meeting with transcription enabled:

  1. Open the meeting chat or calendar event
  2. Click the Copilot icon in the meeting
  3. Or type in the meeting chat: "Summarize this meeting"

Key Prompts for Meetings

Get a summary:

Summarize the key points from this meeting

Extract action items:

What action items were assigned in this meeting? 
List each with the person responsible.

Find specific topics:

What was discussed about the onboarding project?

Catch up on what you missed:

I joined 15 minutes late. What did I miss?

Get decisions made:

What decisions were made in this meeting?

Exercise 1: Summarize a Meeting

Do this now:

  1. Open a recent Teams meeting you attended (that had transcription)
  2. Click the Copilot icon or open the meeting chat
  3. Try these prompts:
Summarize this meeting in 5 bullet points

What are all the action items from this meeting?
Who is responsible for each?

What decisions were made?

What to notice:

  • Copilot pulls from the transcript
  • Action items include speaker attribution
  • You can ask follow-up questions

Catching Up on Missed Meetings

One of the most powerful features: understanding meetings you couldn't attend.

Prompts for Missed Meetings

I missed this meeting. Give me a complete summary including:
1. Key discussion points
2. Decisions made
3. Action items (especially any assigned to me)
4. What I need to know before the next meeting

Was I mentioned in this meeting? What was said?

Were there any action items assigned to the product team?

Exercise 2: Catch Up on a Missed Meeting

Before this exercise:

  • Find a meeting where transcription was enabled (you'll see a transcript icon in the meeting recap)
  • If no such meeting exists yet, use the Exercise 1 meeting summary instead

Note: Copilot can only summarize meetings that were transcribed. Enable transcription by default on your Teams meetings for future use.

Do this now:

  1. Find a meeting you were invited to but didn't attend
  2. Open the meeting recap
  3. Ask Copilot:
I couldn't attend this meeting. Please summarize:
1. What was the main topic?
2. What decisions were made?
3. Are there any action items for me?
4. What do I need to prepare for next time?

Copilot transcribes spoken words. If a presenter shares their screen and points to items without describing them verbally, those details won't appear in summaries.

Section 2: Chat Assistance

Copilot works in Teams chats for quick help while you're messaging.

Accessing Copilot in Chat

  1. Open any Teams chat (1:1 or group)
  2. Click the Copilot icon below the message box
  3. Or type / to see Copilot options

Chat Use Cases for PMs

Summarize a long thread:

Summarize the last 20 messages in this chat

Draft a response:

Help me draft a response to the last message. 
I want to agree but suggest we delay by one week.

Quick research:

What's the current status of the onboarding project 
based on this chat history?

Find information:

When did we last discuss the API integration in this chat?

Exercise 3: Chat Summarization

Do this now:

  1. Open a busy group chat (one with many messages)
  2. Click the Copilot icon
  3. Try:
Summarize what I missed in the last 24 hours

What are the key decisions or action items from recent messages?

Are there any questions that were asked but not answered?

Section 3: Channel Workflows

Enterprise Note: Copilot in Teams channels may be restricted by your tenant configuration, even if Copilot is available in personal chats.

If Copilot is not visible in a channel:

  • Check that Copilot is enabled for that specific channel (requires channel owner or admin action)
  • As an alternative, use Copilot Chat (copilot.microsoft.com) to query channel content
  • Contact your IT helpdesk to request Copilot be enabled for your team's channels

Copilot helps you stay on top of Teams channels without reading every message.

Channel Copilot Features

  1. Open any Teams channel
  2. Click Copilot in the channel header
  3. Ask questions about channel activity

Channel Prompts

Daily catch-up:

What's been discussed in this channel in the last 24 hours?

Find specific topics:

What has been said about the Q1 launch in this channel?

Identify action items:

Are there any open questions or action items in recent posts?

Draft a post:

Help me draft an update post for this channel about 
the onboarding wizard launch. Keep it concise.

Exercise 4: Channel Intelligence

Do this now:

  1. Open an active Teams channel
  2. Click Copilot
  3. Try:
Summarize the important updates from the last week

Draft a quick update post announcing that the 
onboarding wizard is ready for beta testing.
Keep it to 3-4 sentences.

Teams Copilot Best Practices

Do:

  • ✅ Enable meeting transcription for all important meetings
  • ✅ Use Copilot immediately after meetings for follow-ups
  • ✅ Ask specific questions (not just "summarize")
  • ✅ Verify action items with attendees
  • ✅ Use channel summaries for daily catch-up

Don't:

  • ❌ Rely solely on Copilot for critical decisions
  • ❌ Skip meetings assuming Copilot will cover it
  • ❌ Share Copilot summaries without review
  • ❌ Forget that transcription must be enabled
  • ❌ Ignore context Copilot might miss

Prompt Templates for Teams

Save these for quick access:

Meeting Summary Template

Summarize this meeting with:
1. Key discussion points (3-5 bullets)
2. Decisions made
3. Action items with owners
4. Open questions
5. Next steps

Missed Meeting Template

I missed this meeting. Provide:
1. 2-sentence overview
2. Key decisions
3. Action items (especially for me or product team)
4. What I need to prepare for next time

Chat Catch-Up Template

Summarize what I missed since [time/date]:
1. Key updates
2. Decisions made
3. Questions asked of me
4. Action items
