Enterprise Communication Transformation Template

Multi-Audience Communication Prompt

I need to communicate this product update to three different enterprise audiences.

THE UPDATE:
[Paste your core message here]

Please create three versions:

1. **EXECUTIVE BRIEFING** (for steering committee sponsors like the CKO)
   - Lead with metrics and competitive positioning
   - Include: adoption targets, ROI, timeline, risks
   - Reference competitive pressure (McKinsey, BCG)
   - Structured with headers
   - Include "asks" for executive support
   - 1-page maximum

2. **PARTNER COMMUNICATION** (for Practice Leaders like MDs)
   - Focus on business impact (winning deals, finding experts)
   - Lead with time savings and competitive advantage
   - 2 short paragraphs, professional but not formal
   - End with "how to get involved in pilot"

3. **CONSULTANT UPDATE** (for daily users like Senior Consultants)
   - Focus on how it helps their daily work
   - Emphasize: find templates faster, stop recreating work
   - Casual, peer-to-peer tone
   - Include 2-3 specific use cases
   - 3-4 short paragraphs

Change Management Communications

Create change management communications for the AKX AI Search launch:

Core message: [Your feature/change description]

Create versions for:

1. **Awareness Email** (first announcement)
   - What's changing and why
   - How it benefits users
   - Timeline and what to expect
   - Who to contact with questions

2. **Training Invitation** (driving adoption)
   - What users will learn
   - Time investment required
   - How to sign up
   - What they can do after training

3. **Go-Live Announcement** (launch day)
   - It's live - here's how to access
   - Quick start guide (3 steps)
   - Support resources
   - Feedback channels

4. **Reinforcement Message** (2 weeks post-launch)
   - Early wins and success stories
   - Tips from power users
   - Reminder of key benefits
   - Encourage sharing with colleagues

Format each for: [Email / Teams Channel / SharePoint News]

Steering Committee Update

Transform this week's progress into a steering committee update:

Raw updates:
[Paste your status notes, metrics, issues]

Create a structured update:

1. **Executive Summary** (3 bullets, 30-second read)
   - Key progress this period
   - Top metric highlight
   - Primary risk/issue

2. **Key Metrics** (table format)
   - Metric | Target | Actual | Trend

3. **Progress Highlights** (what's going well)

4. **Risks & Issues** (with mitigation)

5. **Decisions Needed** (if any)

6. **Next Period Focus**

Tone: Confident, data-driven, concise.
Length: 1 page maximum.
