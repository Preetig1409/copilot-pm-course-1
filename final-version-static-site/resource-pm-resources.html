PM Frameworks Quick Reference

Frameworks you'll encounter throughout your career:

Strategy & Goals

  • OKRs (Objectives & Key Results) — Set qualitative goals with 3-5 measurable key results. Used by Google, Spotify, LinkedIn
  • Jobs to Be Done (JTBD) — Understand the "job" the customer is trying to accomplish, not just what they say they want

Prioritization

  • RICE (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort) — Data-driven scoring for feature prioritization. Introduced by Intercom
  • MoSCoW (Must, Should, Could, Won't) — Scope management for sprint planning
  • Value vs. Effort Matrix — Simple 2x2 for quick trade-off decisions
  • Kano Model — Distinguish must-haves from delighters based on customer satisfaction

Discovery

  • Design Thinking — Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test
  • Continuous Discovery — Weekly customer touchpoints, opportunity solution trees

Where to find templates: Atlassian's PM guides, Product School's framework library, and ProductPlan's learning center all offer free, downloadable templates.
